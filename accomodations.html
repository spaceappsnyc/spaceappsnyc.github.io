GETTING TO THE EVENT

The International Space Apps Challenge in New York City will be held at Microsoft NYC at 11 Times Square, conveniently located across from Port Authority, an MTA subway and city bus nexus and the central interstate bus terminal of New York City. All major MTA subway lines will get you here: A, C, E to 42nd Street-Port Authority and 1, 2, 3, N, Q, R, 7 to 42nd Street-Times Square. Interstate trains arrive regularly at nearby Penn Station on 34th street, and parking is readily available all over the surrounding area.

HOTEL ACCOMMODATIONS

Our official hotel partner is YOTEL, New York's most innovative hotel located at 42nd and 10th Ave in Hell's Kitchen. Adopting methods of the airline industry, YOTEL makes a visit to NYC affordable, efficient and fun. Guests will enjoy fast kiosk check-in, the world's only robot concierge for storing luggage, and a room designed by first class airline architects.

Freebies include muffins, coffee and tea in the morning and Wi-Fi throughout the entire hotel.

A limited number of NON-REFUNDABLE discount rooms are available for registered hackathon participants on a first-come, first-serve basis. Please email spaceappsnyc@gmail.com for details and availability.
