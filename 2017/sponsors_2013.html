We are Space Apps NYC

A grassroots space hacker community grown from the NASA Space Apps Challenge, founded to improve life on Earth and further the exploration of space. At over 1800 members, we’re the largest regional community of this type in the world, established in 2012 – and we’re creating a next generation space science & technology hub in New York City.

API SPONSOR - SEE SPONSORSHIP GOALS ABOVE

REQUIREMENTS:

  • API evangelist on site for entire event (48 hours); multiple evangelists can take shifts if needed
  • Mentoring teams on site, including brainstorming solutions using API to solve challenges where appropriate
  • Demonstration of API on site, including code samples and examples of use
  • Local access to support documents and technical documentation of API via online or print material

Additional API Sponsor Contributions for Global Access. Not required to participate at site level in NYC, but required at the global level.

  • Global evangelist access for international participants via Skype, livestream, IRC, or other Internet channels
  • Global / Internet access to support and technical documentation for all international participants

BENEFITS:

In addition to sponsorship-level benefits, you will receive the following:

  • Introduction of your company and individual evangelists on global livestream during opening ceremonies
  • Invitation to hold 5 minute API demo on global livestream at the closing ceremonies
  • Opportunity to hand out additional promotional literature to site participants in NYC
  • Opportunity to hang banners at event - IF OFFERING GLOBAL ACCESS, BANNER VISIBLE ON LIVESTREAM

FOR PRIZE SPONSORS: Opportunity to hold raffle and / or present award during Awards ceremony

FOR GIFT SPONSORS: Opportunity for evangelist to introduce company and gifts during Opening ceremony
