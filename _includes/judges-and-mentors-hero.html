Judges and Mentors

Space Apps NYC choses influencers from its space hacker community to judge the Space Apps Challenge. Together they are experts in space science, technology, and entrepreneurship, and will choose New York's global nominees.

Our team of mentors volunteer their time to assist hackers over the weekend in areas of software, hardware, project planning, project presentations, space science, and Earth science. All mentors are available through our online communication tools.
