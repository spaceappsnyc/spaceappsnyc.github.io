THE TEAM BEHIND SPACE APPS NYC

Organizers are ultimately responsible for the hackathon success. They recruit sponsors, secure venues, manage marketing campaigns, schedule speakers, and run the event. All of our organizers are volunteers from the Space Apps community.

Organizing Partners are other organizations who assist in organizing the event.

Sponsors make financial contributions in exchange for recruiting opportunities and a precense at the event.

Partners help market the event and make a variety of non-financial contributions.
