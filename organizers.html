ORGANIZING PARTNERS




The New York Technology Council (NYTECH) is a not-for-profit organization focused on fostering the tech eco-system in New York and a founding partner of Space Apps NYC. We offer highly curated panel discussions, networking events, and CEO roundtables, and advocate on behalf of our membership for government policies that will help tech companies thrive in New York.
www.nytech.org


The StartupBus is an invitation-only group of hackers, designers and business minds as well as an annual competition and a founding partner of Space Apps NYC. Each year 30-35 strangers embark upon a life-changing journey, and in 3 days conceive, build, and launch a startup on a moving bus.
www.startupbus.com


Microsoft (Nasdaq "MSFT")is a 2015 presenting sponsor of Space Apps NYC and the worldwide leader in software, services, devices and solutions that help people and businesses realize their full potential. Microsoft NYC has graciously provided their world class event space for our hackathon, festival, and conference.
www.microsoft.com
